The Studio

Bespoke spaces, built around your narrative.

A studio, not a showroom.

Founded in 2014 by architectural designer Arthur Hargreaves, Aura Design Studio is a boutique interior architecture firm specializing in refined residential sanctuaries and boutique commercial interiors.

Our philosophy is rooted in architectural symmetry, spatial clarity, and a deep respect for organic materials. We balance raw, structural forms with warm plaster washes, custom marble bookmatching, and soft fabrics to compose rooms that feel settled, silent, and premium.

Our Philosophy

Values, drawn in three lines

Spatial architecture

We focus on spatial volumes, natural light, and pathways, ensuring the room’s architecture is perfectly proportioned before layer selection begins.

Tactility & texture

We prioritize organic plaster, wide-plank oak, hand-cut stone slabs, and wool fibers, celebrating natural imperfections.

Digital collaboration

We streamline communication through our client portal, providing real-time timeline tracking and digital material approvals.

Our Values

How we curate

Longevity over trends

We source materials that patinate beautifully over time—brushed brass, hand-finished oak, and unpolished limestones. This ensures your home interior grows richer in character as the years pass.

Fully traceable

Every stone slab and timber block features origin certifications, transparent procurement paths, and direct supply loops.

Bespoke fitting

We design custom furniture and custom light details crafted locally by master specialists in European workshops.

Turnkey logistics

From import regulations to final assembly and styling, our studio manages the entire process so you don't have to worry about schedules or logistics.
