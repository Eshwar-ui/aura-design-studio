Design workflow

The architectural journey

01
Movement 01

Discovery

We align on spatial requirements, lifestyle patterns, lighting angles, and aesthetic visions during our first walk-through.

02
Movement 02

Curation

Our studio curates fabric weaves, timber grains, stone slabs, and layout blueprints, creating a physical and digital board.

03
Movement 03

Visualizing

We output immersive 3D renderings and panoramic walkthroughs so you can experience scale, volume, and shading before build.

04
Movement 04

Reveal

Turnkey delivery. Sourcing, structural coordination, final styling, hanging of art, and custom furniture setup.
