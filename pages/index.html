Our Services

A process, in three movements

We guide clients through a comprehensive design journey — from structural planning to the final styled reveal.

01 Spatial strategy

Architectural space planning

We analyze human traffic, lighting vectors, and functional zones to optimize layouts before selecting finishes. Custom spatial structures are mocked up to test architectural harmony.

  • Custom Furniture Layout Matrices
  • Natural Light & Acoustic Mapping
  • High-End Spatial Partition Detailing
02 The material palette

Bespoke material curation

We curating textiles, stones, woods, and metals. Clients approve physical board samples through their dashboard, complete with sourcing locations and certificate verification.

  • Sourcing Rare European Marbles
  • Organic Wood & Textured Plasters
  • Digital Material Board Approvals
03 The reveal

3D visualisation & styling

Our immersive 3D visualizations offer a realistic rendering of proposed designs before build. Combined with styling lists, clients can preview every cushion, light source, and canvas in space.

  • Panoramic 3D Space Renderings
  • Custom Furniture Selection & Procuring
  • Turnkey Styling & Artwork Placement

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our luxury interior design process.

What is Aura's signature interior design style?

We specialize in Timeless Bespoke Luxury. Our philosophy balances clean architectural geometry with rich tactile layers, such as raw limestone, custom oak cabinetry, and silk-wool bouclé textiles.

How does client portal collaboration work?

Once project onboarding is complete, you receive a unique portal code. Inside, you can review high-definition spatial concepts, view project phase timelines, approve or request edits on material selections, and direct-message our design team.

What is the typical timeline for a full residential project?

A standard full-scope residential commission takes between 12 to 24 weeks. This includes the initial conceptual phase, material procurement, spatial detailing, staging, and the final structural styling.

Do you manage procurement and installation?

Yes. We provide a turnkey design service. This includes purchasing custom European imports, coordinating logistics with transport partners, overseeing custom millwork assembly, and final styling.
