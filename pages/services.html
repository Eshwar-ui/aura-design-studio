Our Services

Architectural interior curation.

Drawn before it's built.

We orchestrate full spatial conversions, managing spatial layouts, technical 3D visualization, finish curation, and direct client collaborations.

Space planning & layout

We optimize spatial flows, configure custom partitions, map lighting corridors, and draft architectural floor plans to ensure structural integrity and seamless movement.

Bespoke material curation

We hand-select premium marble slabs, timber profiles, textured wall plasters, and fabrics, presenting digital and physical boards for client approval.

3D visualisations & styling

We output highly immersive, panoramic 3D space visualizations and photorealistic renderings, providing absolute spatial and finish confidence before build.

The Timeline

A process, in four phases

Our typical 16-week cycle is fully monitored through the client portal.

✓
01 Week 01 – 03

Intake & conceptual layouts

Initial site walkthrough, spatial measurements, lifestyle matrix analysis, and core layout design sketches.

✓
02 Week 04 – 07

Space planning & CAD drafting

Drafting technical blueprints, ceiling plans, electrical layouts, and custom millwork partition diagrams.

✓
03 Week 08 – 11

Material board & 3D render sign-off

Curation of plaster swatches, timbers, stones, and light fixtures. Delivery of panoramic 3D space renders.

•
04 Week 12 – 16

Turnkey execution & furnishing

Sourcing custom European cabinetry, supervising construction placement, arranging light fittings, and final styling.

Queries

Services & billing, explained

What is your fee structure for residential projects?

We bill on a hybrid structure: a fixed design development fee covering spatial drawings and 3D visualization, followed by a commission percentage on procured materials, custom millwork, and styling objects.

Can I keep my own general contractor?

Absolutely. While we provide turnkey build supervision with our trusted build partners, we regularly collaborate with our clients' chosen general contractors and architects. We coordinate site drawings and millwork parameters directly with them.
