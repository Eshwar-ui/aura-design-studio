Onboarding FAQ

What is the first step in your design process?

We begin with a discovery questionnaire followed by an on-site spatial walk-through. This allows us to align on project parameters, lighting vectors, and spatial layouts before design development begins.

Do you work outside of New York?

Yes. We regularly accept residential commissions throughout North America and Western Europe, coordinating logistics and material custom installations with local partners.
